Sink Startup Game

# Game rule:
1. We will play against the Computer.
2. Each player will have 3 boats. The user's boats can be placed by user, while computer's boats
will be generated randomly.
3. Who can guess all the boats of the opponents will win the game!

# Gameplay:

# User vs Computer
1. Choose the game board size.
2. Choose the number of boats
3. Random to choose who go first
